Why CookerCool

Our Core Advantages

CookerCool is a new brand in kitchen, specializing in one-stop, light customized and fast delivery cast iron and enamel cookwares.

With more than 17 years experience in supplying industrial components to Europe and professional technicians, we are implementing a new strategy to enter the kitchen field.

Our foundry is located in South of China for mass production and assembly, while setting one assembly line at Tianjin for customized orders. We provide customers with technical and design service, helping to select the right product for every type of use.
