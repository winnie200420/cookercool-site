About CookerCool

Tianjin New Trend Import & Export Co., Ltd.

CookerCool is a new brand in kitchen, specializing in one-stop, light customized and fast delivery cast iron and aluminum cookwares.

With more than 17 years experience in supplying industrial components to Europe and professional technicians, we are implementing a new strategy to enter the kitchen field.

From the first, we embarked on the road of a virtuous cycle and operating in scale. Our foundry is located in South of China for mass production and assembly there, while setting one assembly line at Tianjin for customized orders.

We provide customers with technical and design service, helping to select the right product for every type of use. Our commitment is directed towards continuous research and developing and production processing, as well as the material testing.

For traditional cast iron cookwares, we improved the roughness of appearance via additional treatment, called "super smooth" cast iron. Also, we supply light-weight cast iron cookwares to improve the user experience.
